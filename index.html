Welcome to my webpage!

Tsukasa Watanabe is a Ph.D. student in political science at the University of North Texas. He receives a BA from Dokkyo University and an MA from Aoyama Gakuin University. His main research interests lie at the intersection of international relations and comparative politics, such as how state formations and shifts in international structures affect the onset and settlement of interstate/civil conflicts. The current research topics include the origins of territorial disputes, diversionary theory, and the relationship between interstate and civil conflicts.
